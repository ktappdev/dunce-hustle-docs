## Does having goons prevent Scrape robberies?

Yes. If you have at least 1 goon, Scrape cash loss is prevented.

However, the protection consumes **1 goon**.

## Can I mug players only in my location?

You can target players outside your location, but being in the same location grants a **10% attack bonus**.

## Why did my travel become so dangerous?

Risk stacks from location risk, your risk modifier, and weapon risk. Snitches and high bounty also increase negative-event pressure, especially CANU bust chance.

## What is the safest way to protect profits?

Bank excess cash in Safehouse and avoid carrying oversized inventory through high-risk routes without defense.

## Why can’t I mug someone even if they are rich enough?

Possible reasons:

- They are hospitalized
- You lack energy
- Anti-grief rule blocks it (target below 20% of your net worth)
- They are below newbie protection threshold

## Why did I get less loot on repeat mugs?

Diminishing returns apply to repeated successful mugs on the same target in the same day: 100% -> 50% -> 25% floor.

## What happens if I forfeit a Plug Out fight?

You lose immediately and the other player wins by forfeit.

## Can a Plug Out end without a winner?

Yes. It can end in a draw if the fight hits the turn cap or times out from inactivity.

## Why was my withdrawal blocked?

Withdrawals are limited to **GYD 50,000 per rolling 24 hours** and must include any mobile banking fee in available bank balance.

## Is Long Form different from regular modes?

Yes. Long Form is persistent-season style with real-time cadence and broader strategic horizon. It rewards sustained risk control and compounding over fast spikes.
