**Dunce Hustle** is a strategic drug trafficking simulation game where players compete to build the highest net worth. You start as a small-time dealer and work your way up by buying low, selling high, and managing risks.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1.  **Start:** You begin with **GYD 10,000** and **24 Energy**.
2.  **Buy:** Purchase goods (Ganja, Coke, etc.) at your current location.
3.  **Travel:** Move to different neighborhoods to find better prices. Traveling costs **Cash** and **Energy**.
4.  **Sell:** Offload your inventory at locations where demand (and prices) are high.
5.  **Survive:** Avoid or defend against random events like police raids (CANU) and robberies (Scrape Heads).
6.  **Upgrade:** Use your profits to buy better **Loadouts** (Weapons, Armor, Goons) to protect your assets.
7.  **Compete:** Check the leaderboard and mug other players to steal their cash and product.

## Key Concepts

- **Net Worth:** Your total score, calculated as `Cash + Inventory Value + Gear Value`.
- **Energy:** Required for travel. Replenishes daily (in-game days).
- **Locations:** Different neighborhoods have different prices and risk levels.
- **Days:** The game progresses in days. Prices fluctuate daily.

## Winning

The game ends after a set number of days (depending on the game mode). The player with the highest **Net Worth** at the end wins.
