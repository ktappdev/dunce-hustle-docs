---
title: Awards
description: End-of-game awards and what you need to do to earn them.
---

Finished multiplayer games can hand out special awards based on how you played, not just whether you won.

## Main Awards

### Biggest Hustler

- Given to the player with the highest final net worth
- **Rarity:** Legendary

### Market Manipulator

- Most trades in the match
- Needs at least **5 trades**
- **Rarity:** Epic

### Street King

- Most successful mugs
- Needs at least **3 successful mugs**
- **Rarity:** Epic

### Snitch Lord

- Most snitches in the match
- Needs at least **3 snitches**
- **Rarity:** Rare

### Survivor

- Best survival rate against CANU events
- Requires at least **3 tracked events**
- **Rarity:** Rare

### Risk Taker

- Most trades made in high-risk locations
- Needs at least **5 high-risk trades**
- **Rarity:** Uncommon

### Ghost

- Fewest PvP interactions
- Usually means **2 or fewer** total mug/snitch involvements
- **Rarity:** Uncommon

### Bankrupt Legend

- For players who went broke during the match but still recovered to finish with meaningful value
- **Rarity:** Legendary

## Why Awards Matter

Awards show up in your long-term profile and separate different playstyles:

- Richest player
- Most aggressive player
- Most slippery player
- Most disciplined trader

They are a second progression track beside pure net worth.
