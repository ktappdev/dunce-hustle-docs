---
title: Bounty & Heat
description: How bounty builds up, why it matters, and how to cool it down.
---

Bounty is the heat on your head. The higher it gets, the more dangerous travel becomes and the more attention you attract.

## How Bounty Increases

### Mugging

Every successful mug adds bounty:

```txt
500 + 35% of cash stolen + 75 per inventory unit stolen
```

That means big robberies spike heat very quickly.

### Getting snitched on

If another player snitches on you, you gain:

- **+1,500 bounty**
- **+0.15 risk modifier**

## Why Bounty Matters

Bounty raises your CANU danger during travel.

Thresholds:

- **GYD 5,000 bounty:** +20% CANU pressure
- **GYD 15,000 bounty:** +50% CANU pressure
- **GYD 30,000 bounty:** +100% CANU pressure

At **GYD 30,000+**, traveling into high-risk neighborhoods can effectively force a CANU bust.

## How Bounty Drops

Bounty naturally comes down over time:

- **Travel decay:** 2% per trip
- **Day rollover decay:** 10% per day

You can cool off, but not instantly. A reckless streak can stay dangerous for several moves.

## Practical Bounty Play

- Push aggression when you have cash banked and strong defense.
- Avoid high-risk travel when you cross a major bounty threshold.
- If you get snitched on, assume your next route is more dangerous than usual.
- Short profit cycles are safer than long inventory holds when your heat is high.
