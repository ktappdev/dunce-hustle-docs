In Dunce Hustle, you aren't just competing on the leaderboard—you can actively hinder your opponents by mugging them.

## Mugging Mechanics

You can attempt to mug another player if:
1.  They are at the same **Location** as you.
2.  You haven't mugged them yet **this game day**.
3.  Their **Net Worth** is above 2000 (Newbie Protection).
4.  You are not trying to mug yourself.

## The Combat Formula

When you initiate a mugging, the game calculates a winner based on stats and a random roll:

```
Attack Score = Attacker Attack + Random(0-20)
Defense Score = Target Defense + Random(0-20)
```

- If **Attack Score > Defense Score**, the Attacker wins.
- Otherwise, the Defender successfully fights off the mugger.

## Rewards & Penalties

If the Attacker wins:

### 1. Cash Stolen
The attacker steals **10-25%** of the victim's cash.

### 2. Goods Stolen
The attacker steals **10-25%** of the victim's inventory.
- *Constraint:* The attacker can only steal what fits in their own inventory.

### 3. Goon Loss
If the victim has any **Goons** in their loadout, they lose **1 Goon** (killed in the fight).

## Strategy
- **Check Leaderboard:** Find rich players to target.
- **Timing:** Mug players after they've sold their goods (high cash) or before they sell (high inventory).
- **Defense:** Invest in Armor and Goons to make yourself a hard target.
