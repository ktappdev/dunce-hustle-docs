Insurance and recovery systems help you survive bad streaks and protect a winning run.

## Insurance Packages

Insurance is bought with cash:

| Package | Price |
| :--- | ---: |
| **1 Day** | GYD 500 |
| **3 Days** | GYD 1,350 |
| **7 Days** | GYD 3,000 |

If you already have active coverage, buying more extends from your current expiry instead of resetting from now.

## What Insurance Protects

### CANU busts

Insurance halves CANU inventory losses:

```txt
insuredLoss = normalLoss * 0.5
```

### Mugging cash loss

Insurance reduces mugged cash loss to **70% of the normal amount** and also tries to leave the victim with at least **GYD 1,000** cash on hand.

It does **not** stop goods theft during a mug.

## Hospitalization

The mugging system supports hospitalization after repeated successful muggings:

- **Threshold:** 5 successful mugs received in the same day
- **Quick-match duration:** 1 day
- **Long-form duration:** 12 hours
- **Hospital destination:** Hospital

While hospitalized, a player is protected from normal actions against them and blocked from normal actions themselves.

## Important Note

Depending on the current version or season settings, hospitalization may be turned off. If that happens, repeated muggings still hurt, but they will not send players into hospital protection.

## Early Discharge

If hospitalization is active and early discharge is available:

- Minimum time before discharge: **3 hours**
- Cost: **15% of current net worth**

This is meant to create a real tradeoff between downtime and preserving leaderboard capital.
