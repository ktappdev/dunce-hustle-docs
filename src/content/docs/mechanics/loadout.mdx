Your **Loadout** determines your effectiveness in combat and your resilience against random events.

## Gear Types

### Weapons
Weapons increase your **Attack** stat, making you more likely to succeed when mugging others.
- *Examples:* Knife, Pistol, SMG.
- *Stats:* Higher tier weapons provide more Attack but may come with higher **Risk** (visibility to police).

### Armor
Armor increases your **Defense** stat, helping you resist muggings and mitigate losses from police raids.
- *Examples:* Vest, Heavy Armor.
- *Stats:* Higher tier armor provides more Defense.

### Goons
Hired muscle to protect your operation.
- **Cost:** Ongoing salary (or upfront cost, depending on game version).
- **Stats:**
    - **+1 Attack** per Goon.
    - **+1 Defense** per Goon.
- **Special Ability:**
    - **Anti-Robbery:** If you encounter a "Scrape Head" event, your Goons will stomp the robber. You lose **0 Cash**, but **1 Goon** is lost in the scuffle.
    - **Meat Shield:** Goons can die during PvP combat if you lose.

## Stat Effects

| Stat | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | Increases chance of winning a mugging attempt. |
| **Defense** | Increases chance of repelling a mugging attempt. Reduces goods lost during a CANU Bust (Police Raid). |
| **Risk** | Increases the likelihood of negative events when traveling (e.g., Police Raids). |

## Managing Loadout

You can purchase gear at specific locations (usually the black market or gun store). Balance your spending between inventory (to make money) and loadout (to protect money).
