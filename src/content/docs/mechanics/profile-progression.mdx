Your profile tracks long-term progress beyond a single run.

## Raw Gold

Every profile starts with **5 dwt** of Raw Gold.

In the UI, Raw Gold is shown in a custom format:

```txt
ounces.remainder RG
```

There are **18 pennyweights per ounce**, so Raw Gold is mostly a profile-status stat right now rather than something you spend in the main game loop.

## Reputation

Reputation is based on your displayed net worth:

```txt
reputation = floor(displayedNetWorth / 50,000)
```

Displayed net worth uses the best of:

- Your lifetime net worth
- Your strongest active net worth

## Level

Your profile level scales from net worth too:

```txt
level = floor(displayedNetWorth / 100,000) + 1
```

That means players climb levels by building and keeping bigger fortunes over time.

## Tracked Career Stats

Your profile also keeps track of:

- Total wins
- Total games played
- Total robberies
- Total snitches
- Lifetime net worth
- Best win streak
- Total awards

## Why This Matters

Profile progression rewards consistency, not just one lucky run. A player who keeps posting strong finishes and avoiding wipeouts will look stronger over time even if they are not always first place.
