Traveling is essential to find the best prices, but it comes with costs and risks.

## Travel Costs

Traveling between locations requires **Cash** and **Energy**.
- **Cash Cost:** Varies by distance (e.g., Stabroek to Ogle costs more than Stabroek to Bourda).
- **Energy Cost:** Calculated as `Cost / 50`. You have a limited pool of energy per day.

## Locations & Risk

Each location has an inherent **Risk Level**, which determines the probability of a bad event occurring when you arrive.

| Location | Risk Chance | Multiplier (Prices) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bel Air** | 5% (Safe) | 1.3x |
| **Stabroek** | 10% | 1.0x |
| **Bourda** | 10% | 1.1x |
| **Eccles** | 10% | 0.85x |
| **Ogle** | 15% | 1.2x |
| **Agricola** | 20% | 0.9x |
| **Albouystown** | 30% (Dangerous) | 0.8x |
| **Charlestown** | 30% (Dangerous) | 0.75x |

*Note: High-risk areas often have the lowest prices, offering a high-risk, high-reward trade-off.*

## Random Events

When traveling, you may encounter one of the following events:

### 1. CANU Bust (Police Raid)
The Customs Anti-Narcotic Unit raids your stash.
- **Effect:** You lose **30%** of your inventory.
- **Mitigation:** Your **Defense** stat (from Armor/Goons) can reduce this loss. Higher defense means fewer goods seized.

### 2. Scrape Head (Robbery)
A local "Scrape Head" attempts to rob you.
- **Effect:** You lose **20%** of your cash.
- **Mitigation:** If you have **Goons** in your loadout, they will "stomp out" the robber. You lose **1 Goon** but save your cash.

### 3. Flood
Specific to **Eccles** (or just heavy rain elsewhere).
- **Effect (Eccles):** Flood damages your goods. You lose **10%** of inventory.
- **Effect (Others):** Heavy rain. No penalty (just flavor text).

### 4. Lucky Find
A rare positive event.
- **Effect:** You find a dropped package containing **5 units** of a random good.
