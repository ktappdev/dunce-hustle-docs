title: Game Constants
description: Core numeric values used by live mechanics.

## Starting Values

- Starting cash: **GYD 10,000**
- Starting energy: **24**
- Max inventory: **1,000 units**

## Economy

- Cheap-location multiplier: **0.7**
- Expensive-location multiplier: **1.4**
- Buy pressure per unit: **0.005**
- Sell pressure per unit: **0.003**
- Price clamp min/max: **0.5x / 3.0x** daily base

## Travel & Events

- Travel energy: `ceil(travelCost / 50)`
- Base CANU share of triggered events: **25%** (before bounty bonuses)
- CANU goods loss base: **30%**
- CANU defense mitigation cap: **80%**
- Scrape cash loss: **20%**
- Flood goods loss (Eccles): **10%**
- Lucky Find gain: **5 units**

## Combat (Mugging)

- Attack energy cost: **10**
- Failed attack cost: **15**
- Newbie protection threshold: **GYD 2,000 net worth**
- Anti-grief minimum target: **20% of attacker net worth**
- Loot ranges: **10%–25%** cash and goods
- Proximity attack bonus: **+10% attack**
- Max goons: **20**
- Failed-attack attacker goon loss chance: **50%**
- Successful-defense target goon loss: **1 goon**

## Banking

- Deposit fee: **5%**
- Mobile banking fee (non-branch): **2%**
- Daily withdrawal limit: **GYD 50,000** per rolling 24h
- Daily bank interest: **3%**
- Daily loan interest: **5%**
- Max loan ratio: **50% of net worth**

## Social Pressure

- Snitch energy cost: **4**
- Snitch risk penalty: **+0.15 risk modifier**
- Risk modifier cap: **0.5**

## Notes

Values can be tuned between versions. Check release notes for balancing updates.
