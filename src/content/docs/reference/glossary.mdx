---
title: Glossary
description: Key terms used throughout Dunce Hustle.
---

## A

**AFK Penalty**  
Energy reduction applied at day rollover if you took no actions that day.

**Attack**  
PvP offense stat from weapon + goons.

## B

**Bank Branch**  
Physical branch location at **Stabroek** where mobile banking fees do not apply.

**Bank Balance**  
Protected stored cash in Safehouse.

**Bounty**  
Heat metric that increases CANU bust pressure.

## C

**CANU Bust**  
Travel event that removes a percentage of inventory.

**Carry Cash**  
Cash currently on hand (not banked).

## D

**Day**  
A round segment where prices remain active until rollover.

**Defense**  
Survivability stat from armor + goons.

## E

**Energy**  
Action resource used for travel, mugging, and social actions.

## G

**Goons**  
Hired crew adding both attack and defense; can be lost in events/PvP.

## H

**Hospitalized**  
Temporary protected state after repeated successful muggings.

## L

**Loadout**  
Your equipped weapon, armor, and goon count.

**Lucky Find**  
Positive travel event giving free random goods.

## M

**Mobile Banking Fee**  
2% fee for banking actions taken outside Stabroek branch.

**Mugging**  
PvP action to steal cash/goods from another player.

## N

**Net Worth**  
Primary score used for ranking and game winners.

## R

**Risk Modifier**  
Temporary additive risk from player actions and pressure.

## S

**Safehouse**  
Banking system for deposits, withdrawals, loans, and interest.

**Scrape**  
Robbery travel event affecting carry cash unless protected by goons.

**Snitch**  
Action that raises a target’s travel risk and bounty pressure.

## V

**Volatility**  
Per-good randomness range used in day price generation.
